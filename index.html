1. Site of employment shall be the residence of employer . Bangladesh Embassy shall be informed of any change of residence within seven (7) days.
2. Contract duration: Three (3) years inclusive of ninety (90) days probation period commencing from employee's departure from the point of origin to the site of employment.
3. Monthly salary to be paid at end of each month by the employer. If requested by the employee, the employer should help the employee to remit the salary into a bank account.
4. Working hours of the employee shall not exceed eight (08) staggered hours per day.
5. Employer shall provide free passage from Bangladesh to the site of employment and upon expiration or termination of contract due to no fault of employee from the site of employment back to Bangladesh.
6. The employer shall ensure that the resident visa/work permit and life and health insurance for the employee is obtained when due without delay, at the expense of the employer (for insurance p12 see 15).
7. The employer shall provide the employee free of charge, suitable and sanitary living quarters as well as adequate food.
8. The employer shall provide all medical services including hospitalization expenses, medicine etc. free and also winter clothing whenever outside temperature is below 20C.
